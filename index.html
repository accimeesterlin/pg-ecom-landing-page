Steps to launching your online business

Find a high-potential product

Find something that has a large global demand, high margins and can be easily warehoused and fulfilled.

Lock down a manufacturer

You need a manufacturer who can keep up with demand and creates a quality product.

Choose a brand name and secure your online presence

Your brand name needs to be not only memorable and appealing to your target audience, but it also needs to be available in the form of a domain name and social media handles.

Develop your website

Shopify is a great choice for those who don’t have the technical know-how or budget to create a custom website.

Create a marketing plan

You have a product and a website, now you need to put your offering in front of consumers who are likely to be interested in the product.

Launch your online store

Do not wait until you think it’s perfect to launch. You will need to continually split-test and make changes -- your e-commerce business will always be growing and evolving.

Who we are

Protege Global Ecommerce (PG-ecom) has trained over 1,200 people in how to launch and manage successful online businesses. Courses are currently offered in Haitian Creole to help Haitians learn how to make money online. Course offerings will be expanded to English soon.

Esterling Accime is a software engineer, educator and entrepreneur with a wealth of knowledge in digital retailing and e-commerce.

Esterling Accime

PG-ecom Founder, United States

Esterling founded Protege Global E-commerce (PG-ecom), a skills training organization that creates content and courses around e-commerce. Courses are provided in Haitian Creole to help Haitians launch, create and manage successful online businesses. Esterling is also co-founder of Ecom Builder that helps customers start their e-commerce business with the right high-conversion creative and assets.

Growing up in Haiti, Esterling has a passion for bringing technology to the country. He believes the future of Haiti’s technology landscape is promising if those with the experience and knowledge democratize access. To help connect Haitians with other technology professionals, Esterling co-founded tichaiti to collectively create solutions that uplift Haiti and the Haitian people.

FAQs

Can I watch the lessons on my phone?

Yes you can watch it on your phone. Navigate via the class url, and login to the assigned courses

How is the material available online?

Online classes are delivered using a learning management system, called Teachable. Students log into Teachable and can select their course sites. Once a student is in their course site, they can access lectures, PowerPoint, assignments, readings, documents, videos, links, comments, etc

Are online classes self-paced or scheduled?

The programs are self-paced or independent study.

Can I go on vacation while taking an online class?

Online courses require weekly logins and weekly assignment submissions. Students must ensure they are able to meet the course requirements throughout the duration of the semester.
